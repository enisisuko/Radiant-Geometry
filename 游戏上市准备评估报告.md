```

    🎵 Chapter1_BGM.ogg         (第一章背景音乐)

  📁 SFX/
    🔊 Player/
      - Jump.wav
      - Dash.wav
      - Attack.wav
      - Hit.wav
      - Death.wav
    
    🔊 UI/
```

  - Incompetech.com (Kevin MacLeod的免费音乐)
